A Decentralized Governance Platform

Choice Coin is an interchain governance platform. Choice Coin enables organizations to make governance decisions in an open and decentralized manner. For the Choice Coin DAO, the voting software Decentralized Decisions leverages Proof-of-Participation as a governance mechanism, allowing voters to have a larger say in direct proportion to their contribution to the network.
